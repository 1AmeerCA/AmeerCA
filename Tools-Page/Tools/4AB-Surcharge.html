📘 Section 4AB – Summary & Key Info

Who: Individuals & Association of Persons (AOPs). Rate: 10% (business/AOP) / 9% (salaried) on the income tax if taxable income exceeds Rs 10 million. Surcharge is calculated on the tax amount, not on income.

Example (TY2026): Salaried individual, income 15M → normal tax ~4.43M, surcharge 9% = 398k, total ~4.83M.

Threshold: Only applies when taxable income > 10,000,000.
